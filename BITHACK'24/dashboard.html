Welcome to BIT HACK'24

ABOUT BIT HACK'24

BIT HACK'24 is an exciting annual hackathon organized by the Bannari Amman Institute of Technology. It brings together enthusiastic developers, designers, and innovators to collaborate and solve real-world problems through creative technology solutions.

Event Highlights

  • 24-hour hacking marathon
  • Workshops and mentoring sessions
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts
  • Prizes for top teams and projects

Organizers

The event is organized by the dedicated team at the Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, with support from industry partners and sponsors.
